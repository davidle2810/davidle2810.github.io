Electrical Vehicle Charging Data¶

In this problem, we will work on the Electrical Vehicle Charging dataset. The dataset is provided in CSV format, consists of multiple data samples. Start_plugin_hour End_plugout End_plugout_hour El_kWh Duration_hours month_plugin weekdays_plugin Plugin_category Duration_category

Below are the variable descriptions:

  • 1 session_ID: Each charging session will be indexed with a unique ID.
  • 2 Garage_ID: Indicates the garage where the charging station is located
  • 3 User_ID: ID of the vehicle
  • 4 User_type: Can only be Private or Shared
  • 5 Shared_ID: For shared user type
  • 6 Start_plugin: The date time when the vehicle start charging
  • 7 Start_plugin_hour: The hour when the vehicle start charging
  • 8 End_plugout: The date time when the vehicle end charging
  • 9 End_plugout_hour: the hour when the vehicle end charging
  • 10 El_kWh: The total electricity (in kWh) for the charging session.
  • 11 Duration_hours: Charging duration in hour.
  • 12 month_plugin: The month of the charging session.
  • 13 weekdays_plugin: The week day of the charging session.
  • 14 Plugin_category: The time during the day.
  • 15 Duration_category: Category for the duration in hour.

Data preprocessing¶

In [8]:
month_mapping = {'Jan': 1, 'Feb': 2, 'Mar': 3, 'Apr': 4, 'May': 5, 'Jun': 6, 'Jul': 7, 'Aug': 8, 'Sep': 9, 'Oct': 10, 'Nov': 11, 'Dec': 12}
month_expr = "CASE " + " ".join([f"WHEN month_plugin = '{month}' THEN {num}" for month, num in month_mapping.items()]) + " END"
weekday_mapping = {'Monday': 2, 'Tuesday': 3, 'Wednesday': 4, 'Thursday': 5, 'Friday': 6, 'Saturday': 7, 'Sunday': 1}
weekday_expr = "CASE " + " ".join([f"WHEN weekdays_plugin = '{weekday}' THEN {num}" for weekday, num in weekday_mapping.items()]) + " END"

df = df.withColumn("session_ID", df["session_ID"].cast(IntegerType())) \
        .withColumn("Start_plugin", to_timestamp(df["Start_plugin"],"dd.MM.yyyy HH:mm")) \
        .withColumn("Start_plugin_hour", df["Start_plugin_hour"].cast(FloatType())) \
        .withColumn("End_plugout", to_timestamp(df["End_plugout"],"dd.MM.yyyy HH:mm")) \
        .withColumn("End_plugout_hour", df["End_plugout_hour"].cast(FloatType())) \
        .withColumn("El_kWh", regexp_replace(df["El_kWh"], ",", ".").cast(FloatType())) \
        .withColumn("Duration_hours", regexp_replace(df["Duration_hours"], ",", ".").cast(FloatType())) \
        .withColumn("month_plugin", expr(month_expr)).withColumn("weekdays_plugin", expr(weekday_expr))
df.show(5)
